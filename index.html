FAQs

Have questions about our services? Find answers to commonly asked questions below.

What services does Eleventhour offer?

Eleventhour provides complete event planning and management for corporate and social events, including pre-event logistics, onsite execution, and post-event follow-up. We handle everything from conferences and galas to themed parties and product launches. Our services also include branding, marketing, and vendor coordination.

Do you handle both corporate and social events?

Yes, we specialize in all event types, from corporate meetings and fundraisers to private celebrations and exhibitions. Each event is tailored to reflect the client’s unique vision and requirements. Creativity and precision define our approach.

Do you organize large-scale events?

Absolutely. We’ve successfully executed large projects like brand launches and public festivals, handling everything from technical setups to crowd management. Scalability and professionalism are at the core of our services.

Do you offer branding and marketing for events and brands?

Yes, we design custom branding, social media strategies, and sponsorship packages. Post-event, we provide media coverage, highlight reels, and PR outreach to amplify your event’s impact.
